About Me

I'm currently working on Vyrtex — we're building the first place people will go to save, share, and discover the best articles on the web. (think of it like Pinterest, but for articles).

Previously, I was the President of Penn Masala, a world-renowned South Asian a cappella group.

I graduated from the Wharton School at University of Pennsylvania last May, where I took classes focused on entrepreneurship, product design, computer science, marketing, and operations.

Projects

Soundscapes

Partnered with @alokedesai to build a multi-platform mobile app that plays music by artists who are originally from your current location.
Winner of the 1st place Microsoft Prize at the Fall 2014 PennApps Hackathon.

RapGenii

A social platform enabling people to work together to create rap lyrics. Built for a class project with @sclarawu and @VonDerpenberg

Evolution of Bollywood Music

Led the creation and marketing of a viral music video highlighting Bollywood classics over the ages. Reached 1,000,000 views in 10 days and led to a performance at the Indian International Film Awards for a live audience of 30,000.
