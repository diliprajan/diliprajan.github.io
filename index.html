Student

Right now, I'm finishing my last semester at the University of Pennyslvania studying entrepreneurship, marketing, and computer science.

Leader
I'm currently the President of Penn Masala, the world's first and premier South Asian a cappella group. Running the group is much like running a business: we regularly go on tour across the U.S. and abroad, manage 50,000 fans on Facebook, and consistently release new music, albums, and videos for our global fanbase. Recently, we performed for an audience of 30,000 at the Indian International Film Awards (the Bollywood Oscars) and are eagerly awaiting our Hollywood debut with a feature in the upcoming movie, Pitch Perfect 2.

Entrepreneur
I'm working on Vyrtex with @tculliver - we're creating a platform to improve the way people can discover, consume, and share articles and other media content that they love. We've conducted research, gathered data from a beta test, and are planning to launch the first public version of the product in February.
